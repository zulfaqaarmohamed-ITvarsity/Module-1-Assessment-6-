Okuma Aventa Baitrunner spinning reels feature on/off auto trip bait feeding system allows anglers to disengage their spinning reel spool and allow their bait to run freely. Slow Oscillation System can improve line lay. Equip with one touch handle with wood knob.

An extra aluminum spool can be a back-up system or for carrying different line sizes.

It features a smooth drag system, a comfortable handle, and a durable construction

A baitrunner reel is a type of fishing reel that is designed to allow the angler to easily switch between two different drag settings. This is useful for fishing in situations where the fish may be more likely to take the bait and run, such as when fishing for larger species or in deeper water.

Baitrunner reels are typically used for freshwater fishing, but they can also be used for saltwater fishing as well.
