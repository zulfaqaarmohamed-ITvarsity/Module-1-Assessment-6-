A spincast reel or closed-faced reel is one of the simplest and most common types of fishing reels. It has a fixed spool that holds the line. Unlike best baitcasting reels, there is no backlash.

A spincast reel is an easy-to-use, versatile fishing reel that can be used for a variety of different fishing techniques. The spincast reel is perfect for anglers who are just starting out,and it can also be used by experienced anglers in certain situations
